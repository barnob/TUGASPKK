Synopsis

Supergirl is an American television series developed by Ali Adler, Greg Berlanti and Andrew Kreisberg (two who have previously created Arrow and The Flash) that originally aired on CBS and premiered on October 26, 2015. The film is based on the DC Comics character Supergirl. Supergirl is a costumed superheroine who is Superman's cousin and one of the last surviving Kryptonians.
